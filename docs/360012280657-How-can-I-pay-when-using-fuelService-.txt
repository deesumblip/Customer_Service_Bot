How Can I Pay When Using Fuelservice When using fuelService, you can pay via card or cash, or through Pay at Pump mobile payment at participating Shell Stations. The fuelService app and Shell App work together to let fuelService customers Pay at Pump from inside their car. All you have to do is download the Shell App and set up an account with Shell Go+, then select pay at pump from within the fuelService app and you will be taken to the Shell app to pay.
