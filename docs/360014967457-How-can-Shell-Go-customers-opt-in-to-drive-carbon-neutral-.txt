How Can Shell Go Customers Opt In To Drive Carbon Neutral All Shell Go+ customers can offset the carbon emissions from their fuel by opting in. To do so,download the Shell app and tap on the rewards page icon. You then need to click on the DriveCarbon Neutral section at the top and slide across the button to opt-in and Drive CarbonNeutral. If you dont have the Shell app you can contact our customer operations team here to opt-in, you just need your Shell Go+ number. We are also working on ways to enable card customers to opt-in via the website in future.
