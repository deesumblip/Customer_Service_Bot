How Does Pay At Pump Work At A Shell Station To use Pay at Pump at a Shell Station follow these steps: Park at the pump and switch off the engine Launch the Shell App from your mobile, tap Pay at Pump Enter your pin code or use touch ID Tap the yellow bar to start the app will locate your Shell Station Once your location is confirmed, enter your pump number the app will be Authorising your account Exit your vehicle and Start fuelling your tank (put your mobile somewhere safe while fuelling) From the comfort of your vehicle, check your mobile the transaction will be on screen A receipt will be sent to your email address. Now youve filled up, youre ready to go.
