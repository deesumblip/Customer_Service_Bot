Is There A Limit To The Number Of Visits Per Day That Can Be Credited To My Account Earning Visits. A maximum of 3 qualifying visits per day can be credit to your account. These visits will need to meet the minimum spend required for fuel and/or non-fuel related products. Please note, if 2 or more visits occur within 10 minutes of each other both transaction will be amalgamated and will count as one qualifying visit. Redeeming rewards. Member benefits and rewards can be redeemed in up to 5 visits each day.
