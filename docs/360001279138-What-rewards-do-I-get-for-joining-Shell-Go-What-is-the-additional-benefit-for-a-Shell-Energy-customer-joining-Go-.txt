What Rewards Do I Get For Joining Shell Go What Is The Additional Benefit For A Shell Energy Customer Joining Go Shell Go+ members enjoy these rewards: Member benefit 10% savings across hot drinks, deli2go, Jamie Oliver deli by Shell, oils and car wash Money off fuel every 10 visits (the value of this reward is based on what you've purchased during these visits, either in the shop or on fuel) Surprise freebies chosen exclusively for you Additionally, Shell Energy members who join Shell Go+ will receive 3% off 60 litres of fuel per month. Please note that the value fuel reward you receive after 10 visits is based on what you have spent in both the shop and on fuel during your visits. So it is not the case that if you spend 100 on fuel every visit you will get the same reward as someone who spends 10 on fuel every visit.
