What Is The Shell App And How Can It Benefit Me The Shell app has the three great features to keep you on the move: Shell Go+ helping you enjoy rewards every time you visit like 10% off hot drinks and a range of food*, money off fuel every 10 visits and surprise freebies, all on your phone. Pay at pump letting you pay for fuel on your app from the comfort of your car. Station locator use the app to find your nearest service station, wherever you are! Theres a quick navigation bar at the bottom of the screen, which puts your Shell Go+ card at the heart of the app so its always easy to find.
